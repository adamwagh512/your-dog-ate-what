Frequently Asked Questions

What does this site do?

The purpose of this site is to enable dog owners to quickly determine whether the substance their dog ate was toxic and whether they need to see a vet.

How do I use this site?

Type what your dog ate into the search box and click the submit button below that box. Your results will pop up in a window that says how potentially hazardous the consumed item is. If we think a vet visit is necessary, we will provide information with for the nearest open veterinary clinics.

Who are we?

We are a group of dog-loving, coding students who came together with one simple goal in mind: Make the world a better place for dogs!

Why did we build this site?

To make the world a better place for dogs! People around the world love their pets; some people even consider them to be family members. This web site is designed to help people keep their dogs safe and healthy.

I know a toxic substance that is not in your poison index. Can you add it?

Yes we can! Send an email message to yourdogatewhat@gmail.com, and we'll add it when we get the chance.

How did we build the site?

We built this page using a variety of APIs and frontend web technologies. For more information, take a look at our GitHub repo.
